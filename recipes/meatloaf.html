Meat Loaf

Description

This is the best ever meatloaf recipe. It's one of my favourite dishes that I have found and I cannot wait for you to try it!

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 sliced bread, crumbled
  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrot
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
